Direction 3 — Warm Human

A softer, approachable register that de-stresses the auth moment: warm off-white canvas, fully rounded pill buttons and large radii, a friendly amber-to-coral accent, and a slightly heavier, welcoming type weight. Motion is implied to be gentle and lifting (cards rise on hover) rather than mechanical. Brand color lives in two tokens so a tenant can warm or cool the accent without losing the rounded, human shape language.
